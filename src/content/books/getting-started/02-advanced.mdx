## Warning the Reader (Gracefully)

Sometimes you need to grab the reader's attention quickly. Maybe a dependency is deprecated, or there is a security vulnerability they should be aware of. Instead of bold text, we use a universal `<Callout />` component.

<Callout type="note">
  **Did you know?** The latest version of Astro lets you use plain `markdown`
  alongside `mdx` files seamlessly within your collections.
</Callout>

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip: Use TypeScript">
  When writing complex framework components, leveraging TypeScript can heavily
  reduce run-time errors by catching prop validation issues early.
</Callout>

<Callout type="warning" title="Breaking Change">
  After migrating from Astro v3 to v5, ensure your `content.config.ts` has been
  correctly updated to use `astro:content`.
</Callout>

<Callout type="danger" title="Heads up">
  Never commit secrets to your repository. Use environment variables and a
  secrets manager.
</Callout>

## Mathematics (KaTeX)

### Calculus

**Taylor Series Expansion:**

$$
f(x) = f(a) + f'(a)(x-a) + \frac{f''(a)}{2!}(x-a)^2 + \frac{f'''(a)}{3!}(x-a)^3 + \cdots
$$

**Fourier Transform:**

$$
\mathcal{F}\{f(t)\} = F(\omega) = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} f(t) e^{-i\omega t} \,dt
$$

**Inverse Fourier Transform:**

$$
f(t) = \frac{1}{2\pi} \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} F(\omega) e^{i\omega t} \,d\omega
$$
